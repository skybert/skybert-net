My top things to do in Oslo list

  1. The annual Oslo Music Party festival . Free concerts all day throughout the city. Each stage has its own theme or music genre. Sit in the grass in your favourite park and listen to new bands every half an hour.
  2. The annual River walk Walk along the Aker river at night, with candles leading the way and choirs, theatre groups and street musicians entertaining you ever inch of the way.
  3. Every Sunday night, Blå is the only place to be where the ubiquious Frank Znort Quartet gives you a flabbergasting blues, jazz, funk, reggae & rock 'n roll experience for free.
  4. For the price of a single fair ticket, you can enter one of the ferries that shuttle between the isles in the Oslo fjord. The isles are great places to go for big city relief, to barbecue, play volleyball, swim or - if you're into that sort o thing - tan.

    Alternatively, you can simply stay on the ferry and not get of at any of the islands. Your very own, fjord cruise!

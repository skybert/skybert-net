A Day in the Sign of Music

Yesterday, I spent a whole day in the sign of music. Oslo was the venue for the annual Musikkfest festival. All over town there were venues with different music themes such as rock, pop, singer/songwriter, reggae, hip hop, metal & choir. The sky was blue, the sun was shining, perfect Norwegian summer temperature and thousands of merry people.

Each band performed for half an owner, giving you a huge number of experiences over the course of the day. I got at least nine hours worth of concerts yesterday. The first concert being at 1300 and the last one starting at 0130!

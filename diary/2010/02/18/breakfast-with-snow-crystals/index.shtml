breakfast with snow crystals

I've just finished my breakfast. Yann Tiersen is playing in the background while I read some good news articles while having the aftertaste of the coffee in my month.

Meanwhile, the snow is coming down as big snow crystals outsidde my window. They play, chase each other back and forth. The roof tops are all white, some chimneys have a tall snow hat on top of them.

Winter is great.

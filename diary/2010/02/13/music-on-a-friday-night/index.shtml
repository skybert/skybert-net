Music on a Friday Night

Last night, I visited the Winter Wake Up festival in Oslo. As I never had heard any of the bands before I ventured into the concert halls free from expectations (with the notable exception of enthuastic friends' recommendations of course).

I was impresed by the beauty of Ellen Miles' voice and blown away by the funky music and extremely tight performance of Ole Børud and his band (I cannot believe he's not a number one music celebrity in Norway!). Genre wise, Humming People was the band that clicked the best with me and is probably the band from this evening I'll play the most on my CD player (well music player, to be brutally honest, but yeah).

All in all a great music feast, all in the comfort the centre of Oslo.

first bike ride of the year

Yes, I know it's late and you all have probably gotten into gear with your bikes, but for me, yesterday was the venue for the first ride of the year!

It went surprisingly well (yes, my bum does hurt) and revisiting my favourite places in the forest was like getting back home. There's been 1-1.5 months since I went passed these same trees. Then on skis, now on bike.

There's still some patches of snow on the ground, and the further you get into (and up) in the forest, the more there is of it. Still, it didn't cause any problems, even the trails I tried were more than bikeable (a wee bit wet & muddy admittedly, but still).

Man, life is great!

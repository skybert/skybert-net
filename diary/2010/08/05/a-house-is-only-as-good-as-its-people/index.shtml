A House Is Only As Good As Its People

The quote is from a friend of mine. He explained why enjoyed having me staying at his place when I didn't have a place of my own.

The last couple of days, or indeed weeks, I've had a lot of friends over. Some have been stayed over for a night or two, some have come for lunch, some for dinner, a glass of whiskey, some darn good conversations and some for a good song and a couple of groovy guitar cords.

I must say I like having guests. My home feels so alive when it's full of people, laughter, high spirits, smells, music and lively discussions. A house is only as good as its people, I totally agree!

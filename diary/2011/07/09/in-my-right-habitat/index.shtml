In My Right Habitat

An inevitable side effect of living in Taiwan, is that I constantly am faced with every day life shortcomings: I cannot read this, I cannot say that and I cannot understand those words.

With that backdrop, it was wonderful to join a mountaineering group for a two day hike in the mountains. Here, I could relax doing things I knew how to do. I "understood" the "language" and culture of mountaineering: crossing a forest river, climbing on slippery stone, setting up a tent, walking in the forest. I believe everyone need to get back to their "home turf" every once in a while. I for one do.

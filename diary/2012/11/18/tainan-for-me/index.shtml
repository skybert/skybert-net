Tainan for Me

Tainan for me, is countless small restaurants on every corner with delicious, traditional food.

Tainan for me, is thousands of friendly people smiling at me when I walk through the streets and alleyways.

Tainan for me, is beautiful sunrises and magnificent sunsets.

Tainan for me, is old historic sights like Eternal Golden Castle (億載金城) and Chihkan Tower(赤崁樓) telling the fascinating story of Taiwan.

Tainan for me, is the gigantic Tainan Flower Night Market (花園 夜市) with more dishes than my stomach can muster.

Tainan for me, is loud and colourful parades through An Ping (安平區).

Tainan for me, is amazing temples in every street, in every ally.

Tainan for me is where the restaurants serve the best food and fruits in Taiwan and where the staff not only serve you, but touch your heart with their sincere wish to be good to you.

Tainan for me, is where strangers transform into friends faster than any place I ever have been.

Tainan for me, is relaxing with a papaya milk under the big trees in Confucius temple park (台南孔廟).

Tainan for me, is my second home on the other side of the world.

Uncountable Nouns

Watch the uncountable nouns, e.g. no plural of content ("the contents of the book" is different from "edit two contents", here it should rather be "edit two content items").

Here are some more "favourite" uncountable noun mistakes:

  • Will we modify the codes directly? : Software source code is always written in singluar: code.
  • A lot of stuffs : stuff is never written in plural
  • Two breads : bread is never written in pluar)
  • Three peoples : three peoples mean that there are three large ethnic/tribal groups of people. Normally, you just want to say people in singular.
  • Logics : logic is never written in pluarl, rather use: A lot of logic, or complex logic, much logic,

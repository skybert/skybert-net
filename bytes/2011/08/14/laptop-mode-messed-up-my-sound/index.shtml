laptop mode messed up my sound

To my astonishment, I discovered today that laptop-mode-tools crewed up my sound. For some reason, on my Thinkpad X300, laptop-mode-tools makes my sound completely messed up. I don't know how to describe it, but it sounds like there's three screws loose from my soundcard and there's too much vibration in the sound when high and low tones are struck.

I have no idea what exactly it is with laptop-mode-tools on my system, but somehow it messes up the sound completely. Luckily, I found out and after removing the package, I can again enjoy good music on my dear laptop.

Getting the Microphone to Work on Intel 82801I

I had to add the following to /etc/modprobe.d/alsa-base.conf in order to get the microphone on a Lenovo Thinkpad Edge: 

options snd-hda-intel model=laptop

The drawback of this solution, is that plugging in earphones or speakers in the audio jack doesn't work anymore. However, this was the only way I could get the built in microphone to work.

The full details of the sound card is as follows: 

00:1b.0 Audio device: Intel Corporation 82801I (ICH9 Family) HD Audio Controller (rev 03)

Other system details which may be useful for you: 

$ lsb_release -r -i
Distributor ID: Ubuntu
Release:        11.04
$ uname -r 
2.6.38-11-generic

What To Do If Phionvpn Is Slow

Sometimes, I've seen my Phion VPN being unusually slow. You might think, with good reason, that it's due to the local network connection or the up link at your company site. Although this might be true, I've normally found Phion VPN slowness being due to old phionvpn, lurking around my system making the VPN client constantly re-connect.

To verify if this is the case with your connection, go to the status page on your VPN client. If everything is fine, the status should constantly be a green CONNECTED string. However, if you've got several clients running, this will constantly show various messages, most telling RECONNECT_PENDING

To remedy this (tested both on Linux and Mac OS X), open a terminal and execute: 

# killall phionvpn

Then start phionvpn again and verify that the connection status keeps itself on the CONNECTED mark. The connection to your internal addresses should now be noticably faster.

Also note, that in my experience, you should not run two phionvpn instances on two different computers on your away/home office network as the instance may cause problems for the other.

Loving the GMail Interface

I love the GMail user interface. It's great. And the more I dwelve into it, the greater it becomes. Some of the features I've come to appreciate lately include:

  • Super easy creation of mail filters
  • It understands that a posting belongs to a mailing list, which again caters for even easier creation of mailing lists.
  • Surprisingly good support for shortcuts, including Vi-like navigation in the inbox.
  • Automatically adds meeting invitations (even the ones that come from Microsoft Outlook) to your Google Calendar.

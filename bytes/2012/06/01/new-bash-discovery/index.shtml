New BASH discovery

I just discovered an interesting thing in BASH: normally, you have to dereference a variable by a dollar character, as in: 

name="Lisa"
echo "Hi" $name

However, when you're inside a $(( ... )) construct, you don't have do use the dollar in front of variables! For instance: 

age=100
echo "Half of the age is" $(( age / 2 ))

BASH never seizes to amaze me!

Creating Video Slideshow

I have never had the need for such an application before now, but found to my great surprise that there exists several good specialised programs for this on Linux.

My first attempt with Photofilmstrip 1.4.0 was a bad one however, as it crashed when I tried to import 375 MBs worth of pictues.

My second attempt with Imagination 3.0 was much better. Here, everything worked flawlessly, handling my large picture set without any problems, simple nice UI and few, but essentials options.

I love programs like this! Nice, user friendly, simple, stable and works with large amounts of data. Excellent!
